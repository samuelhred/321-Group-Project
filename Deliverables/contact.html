About Us

The Tuscaloosa Trade Fair Association is dedicated to promoting local businesses and artisans through our annual trade fair events. Our mission is to create a vibrant marketplace that connects vendors with the community while fostering economic growth in the Tuscaloosa area.

Our History

Founded in 2025, the Tuscaloosa Trade Fair Association has grown from a small local event to a premier destination for vendors and shoppers alike. We pride ourselves on creating an inclusive environment that celebrates the diversity and creativity of our community.

Our Values

  • Supporting local businesses and artisans
  • Creating opportunities for economic growth
  • Fostering community connections
  • Promoting sustainable business practices
